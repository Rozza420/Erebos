Multi-Modal Texts

Quotes

Quotes and themes and the way they affect the book

Chapter 1:
‘It was like throwing crackers at a can of petrol’ (simile) this quote was a description of the temper of a girl called Helen. This shows that her character in the game could be a dangerous one and this acts as foreshadowing. The theme is rage.

Chapter 5
“They serve you. They make the impossible possible. They make your dreams come true” This is foreshadowing the importance of a mythical item inside the game. This could become Nicks mission, to find the wish crystals

Chapter 6:
‘Time flows like water’ (simile) It explains that life keeps going on around them when they are playing the game and they can’t stop it. The theme is inevitability

Chapter 4:
‘ Makes the cheekbones more pronounced and…’ (imagery and allusion to royalty) this allusion is an indicator as to how Nick wants to be perceived. It also shows how the game makes people feel. The theme is power.

Chapter 3:
‘What is it like to be dead?’(question/ interrogative language) it shows that the game is able to answer any question in abstract ways. The theme is curiosity.

Chapter 1:
‘Was he just imagining all this conspiracy stuff?’ (rhetorical question) Nick knows something is going on but no one will tell him and Collin just says it’s a Linkin Parks CD. the theme is mystery.
